# Prompts for Learning English with ChatGPT

ChatGPT, İngilizce öğrenmek için güçlü bir araç olabilir — özellikle bir dil ortağı
gibi pratik yapmanız, dilbilgisi kurallarını özümsemeniz veya kelime dağarcığınızı
genişletmeniz gerektiğinde. Aşağıda farklı amaçlar için kullanabileceğiniz hazır
prompt örneklerini bulabilirsiniz.

---

### Zaman kiplerinde pratik

> I want you to help me learn English, you will give me an example using the tenses in
> English including subcategories without any explanation, and ask me which tense the
> sentence belongs to, after my answer even if I know or don't know, you will write the
> correct tense for me and give me another example.

---

### Kelime öğrenme ve quiz

> I want to improve my English vocabulary. Give me 5 intermediate-level English words
> I probably don't know yet, with their definitions, example sentences, and Turkish
> translations. After showing them, quiz me by giving sentences with blanks and asking
> me to fill in the correct word.

---

### Gramer düzeltme

> Act as an English grammar corrector. I will write sentences in English and you will
> correct any grammar mistakes, explain what was wrong and why, then give me a similar
> sentence to practice writing correctly.

---

### Yazma pratiği

> I want to practice writing in English. Give me a topic to write about (3–5 sentences
> are enough). After I write, correct my grammar and vocabulary, suggest improvements,
> and rate my writing from 1 to 10 with a brief explanation.

---

### Konuşma simülasyonu

> Let's have a conversation in English. You will play the role of a native English
> speaker. Correct my mistakes naturally within the conversation, and if I make a
> significant error, point it out after your reply with a brief explanation.

---

### Deyim ve kalıplar

> Teach me 3 common English idioms or collocations used in everyday conversation.
> Explain what each one means, give two example sentences for each, and tell me a
> situation where I might use them.

---

### Dinleme ve anlama testi

> Write a short paragraph (5–6 sentences) at B1 level English about any everyday topic.
> Then ask me 3 comprehension questions about it. After I answer, tell me which ones
> I got right and explain the correct answers for the ones I missed.

---

> **Kullanım ipucu:** Promptları olduğu gibi yapıştırabilir ya da seviyenize göre
> "beginner / intermediate / advanced" gibi eklemeler yaparak özelleştirebilirsiniz.
